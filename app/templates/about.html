We make links simpler.

Shortly was built to remove friction from sharing links — clean URLs, clear insights, and calm design.

What we do

Shortly is a modern URL shortening platform designed for people who value clarity over clutter. We help individuals and teams transform long, complex URLs into short, reliable links that are easy to share and easy to track.

From personal projects to professional campaigns, Shortly keeps your links organized, accessible, and under control.

  • ⚡ Fast link creation
  • 🔒 Secure and reliable redirects
  • 📊 Meaningful click insights
  • 🎯 Clean, distraction-free UI

Our principles

Simplicity

Software should reduce complexity, not introduce it.

Trust

Your links are critical. We treat reliability and security as non-negotiable.

Clarity

Clear data, clear design, clear decisions.

Why Shortly exists

We noticed that many link shorteners focused on features but forgot about experience. Dashboards were cluttered, actions felt heavy, and simple tasks took too much effort.

Shortly was created to be different — thoughtful design, purposeful features, and a workflow that respects your time.

Looking ahead

Our vision is to become a calm, dependable layer between your content and your audience — where every link feels intentional.

We believe productivity tools should feel invisible, not overwhelming.
